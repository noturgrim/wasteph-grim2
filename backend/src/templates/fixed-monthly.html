DISPOSAL PROCESS

Garbage collected from your facility shall be disposed of at Prime Waste Solutions, Inc's Material Recovery Facility, located at Sitio Kainsikan, Brgy. Binaliw, Cebu City. All garbage collected and disposed shall be issued a Certificate of Disposal from Prime Waste Solutions, Inc.

Processes:

Upon arrival of our trucks at the MRF, the solid waste shall undergo the following processes:

  1. Sorting: Sorting facility where collected waste is meticulously segregated into categories such as plastics, paper, glass, metals, and organic waste. This step is crucial for effective recycling and further processing.
  2. Recycling: Items marked as recyclable such as plastics, paper, glass, and metals, shall be collected by accredited recyclers from the premises of Prime Waste Solutions.
  3. Waste to Energy: Organic waste is used to fuel the waste-to-energy technologies to convert non-recyclable waste into energy. This method helps minimize the volume of waste sent to landfills while producing renewable energy, contributing to the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions. Prime Waste Solutions has started the production of municipal waste solid-derived fuel in Cebu. This fuel is a low-carbon refuse derived fuel (RDF) produced from Municipal solid waste derived from its daily 1000 tons of waste from nearby Cities, Municipalities, and Barangays.
